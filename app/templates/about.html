About Dokkai

Welcome to Dokkai. The aim of the platform is to be an all-in-one language platform for learners who want to be consistent and make real progress but may struggle with the overwhelming amount of tools and courses online. The core of the app is the flashcard system scheduled using the FSRS algorithm with everything else being built around this.

Core Features

  • FSRS-powered flashcards for optimal review timing
  • Grammar lessons written to be clear and straight to the point
  • Pre-made decks that can be imported for study on specific topics
  • Kana Guesser for those just starting out
  • XP systems and leaderboards to boost motivation

Recommendation

  • Do your reviews every day when possible to keep the workload small
  • Start with a manageable number of new cards and gradually build up to bigger numbers
  • Mix flashcards with native content (reading, watching tv shows, etc.) for the best results

For more details on everything, check out: How to Use
